My Story

Joe Malone is a 2007 High School graduate of Marble Falls, TX. Shortly after graudation, Joe enlisted in the US Navy where he obtained a rating of Aviation Electronics Technician-Organizational Level. Joe served assigned to Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron Nine (HSC-9), located in Norfolk, VA. Here he learned important skills in the area of troubleshooting: Critical thinking, flexibility, and leadership are only a few. He received several commendations for excellence in the performance of his duties, whereby, in four short years, Joe successfully advanced to the rank of E-5. In September 2013, upon completion of his enlistment contract, Joe was honorably discharged, and promptly moved back home to Texas.

Once discharged from the Department of Defense, Joe entered into the world of Metrology, hiring on with Hexagon Manufucturing Intelligence as a Field Service Engineer. In this role, Joe's territory included: Texas, Louisiana, Arkansas, and Oklahoma. His primary duties were to install, calibrate, and repair Coordinate Measurement Machines(CMMs), with a special emphasis on the Italian 'DEA' Model of Machine. He gained advanced knowledge of geometric mapping for bridge and gantry style machines, with the use of Laser and other calibrated reference standards. As his experience increased and his abilities improved he was tasked with training and instruction of new hires.
